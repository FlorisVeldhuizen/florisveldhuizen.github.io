To finalize the test, I will disclose some information about the test design. The reviews shown in the beginning of the test were presented as if they were real. However, they were fabricated. The reason that this was done was to get a non-biased result, to get your reaction as if you were browsing online (and not participating in a user test). This is important to get realistic data, which tells more about online behaviour.
