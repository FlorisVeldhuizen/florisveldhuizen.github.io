How to use Picobelly

Find a meal online

Pick a delicious homemade meal that you like in our Facebook group.

Reserve & pay

Pay beforehand to reserve a meal for you.

Pick up your meal!

Pick up your homemade meal at the promised date and time.

Show some love!

If you liked it, send a message to the cook or leave a small review! ❤️

Feel like cooking?

Everything starts with a great meal idea. What would you like to cook?

Getting ready!

Wait until enough people have reserved your homemade meal before you do your groceries!

Prepare your dish

Get crazy in the kitchen and create something delicous!

Hand out your meal!

Make your guests happy and give them your homemade meal!
